Career Advisory and Augmentation Services

KIIT has a dedicated Career Advisory and Augmentation Services department takes care of the holistic development of the students for Placement Training and also takes care of shaping careers post their graduation by training them for higher studies.

Present and past students may contact the Help Desk for issues relating to Training & Placement.

The Help Desk In-charge will resolve the issues on the spot as far as possible. If the issue deserves detailed attention, he / she will intimate the same and request an E-mail to be sent.
